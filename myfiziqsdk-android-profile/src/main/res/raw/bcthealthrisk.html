BCT Health Risk

BCT Health Risk categorization is determined by predicted body fat percentage and the location of that fat on the body.

Excess body fat combined with a wider waist can be indicative of a heightened level of visceral adipose tissue which is linked to increased risks of chronic disease and mortality.

Low Risk

Body fat percentage and body measurements do not indicate an increased level of metabolic health risk.

Medium Risk

Body fat percentage and body measurements indicate a medium risk of metabolic health issues.

High Risk

Body fat percentage and body measurements indicate a high risk of metabolic health issues. This could be due to excess fat or a preferential storage of excess body fat around the waist, which has been strongly linked with increased metabolic complications.

Very High Risk

Body fat percentage and body measurements indicate a very high risk of metabolic health issues. This could be due to excess fat or a preferential storage of excess body fat around the waist, which has been strongly linked with increased metabolic complications.
